The ancient gateway hums as you step forward, its carved runes shifting like ripples on water. Beyond lies a realm where the elements reign supreme—winds howl through jagged peaks, rivers carve twisting paths through stone, fire cracks and sputters in unseen forges, and the very earth trembles beneath your feet. Four trials stand before you, each bound to the forces of nature. To pass, you must harness their power—or be consumed by it.
